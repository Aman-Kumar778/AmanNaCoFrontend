Cyan Magenta Yellow (CMY)

CMYK is an subtractive reflected light color model.where each color is stored in one byte.

All possible combinations of CMYK or Cyan Magenta Yellow and Key(Black) give 232 = 4,294,967,296 different colors.

Starting with White (color), to which different color inks are added to absorb(subtract) light that is reflected.
