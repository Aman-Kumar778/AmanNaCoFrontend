About

Welcome to the "Color Models" website. This platform serves as a guide to understanding various color models and their applications in design and development. Each page is designed to provide detailed insights into different aspects of colors and how they are represented.

About the Pages

  • Home: Introduces the concept of color models and their significance in web design and graphics.
  • RGB: Explains the RGB color model, which combines red, green, and blue light to create a wide spectrum of colors.
  • CMY: Focuses on the CMY (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) subtractive color model used in printing.
  • Web Safe Colors: Describes the 216 web-safe colors that ensure consistency across all devices and browsers.

About the Internship
