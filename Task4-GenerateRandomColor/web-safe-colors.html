Web Safe Colors

Web Safe Colors consist of 216 colors that are designed to appear consistent across all devices and web browsers, even those with limited color capabilities. These colors are created by using combinations of the hexadecimal values 00, 33, 66, 99, CC, and FF for the red, green, and blue components of a color.

Benefits of Web Safe Colors

  • Ensure consistency across various devices and browsers.
  • Provide a predictable and non-dithered display.
  • Ideal for websites targeting older systems or limited color monitors.
